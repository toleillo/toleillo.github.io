Privacy Policy

For Animals Multiadventure

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

This privacy policy describes the practices of Animals Multiadventure ("we", "us", "our") regarding information in our mobile application. Your privacy is our priority. Our game has been designed so you can enjoy it without worrying about your personal data, as we do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

1. Data We DO NOT Collect

We do not ask you to register or provide any personal information to play. We do not collect data such as your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or precise location. The game is fully functional without this information.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of our game displays ads to support its maintenance. We use the Google AdMob advertising service.

What data may AdMob collect?

To show you relevant ads, AdMob may collect and use anonymous data, such as your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), your approximate IP address, and information about your interaction with the ads.

What is it used for?

This data is used to limit the frequency with which you see the same ad, to display advertising that may be of interest to you, and to measure the effectiveness of campaigns.

We do not have access to this information. The collection and use of this data are governed by Google's own privacy policy. We recommend you review it for more details: Google Privacy Policy.

3. In-App Purchases

We offer the option to make a single in-app purchase to remove all ads. All transactions are securely processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, receive, or store any payment data, such as your credit card details or bank account information. All that information is managed exclusively by Apple.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not target our game to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction) and do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information.

5. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
